Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how DentaCare ("the App") handles your information. The App is developed and operated by pdlsaktipur-cyber.

1. Information We Collect

DentaCare is a dental clinic management application. The App may collect and store the following information locally on your device:

  • Patient names and contact details
  • Dental treatment records and prescription history
  • Appointment information

2. How We Use Your Information

All data entered into the App is stored locally on your device using browser storage (IndexedDB/localStorage). We do not collect, transmit, or share your data with any third-party servers or services.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal data with third parties. Data shared via WhatsApp or other messaging platforms is done solely at the user's discretion.

4. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device. You are responsible for the security of your device and the data stored within it.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by healthcare professionals. It is not directed at children under the age of 13.
